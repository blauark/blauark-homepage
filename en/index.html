About Us

About BlauArk

BlauArk, whose name means “Ark on the blue sea,” was founded to act as a bridge of trust linking Korea with the world. From cutting-edge electronics to agricultural products and everyday goods, we supply a wide range of Korean products to the global market and build safe, efficient supply chains through close partnerships with our local partners.

As a specialised trading company, we ensure stable procurement and accurate customs clearance from raw materials to finished goods, and provide customised logistics services. Based on abundant experience and expertise in trade, customs and logistics, we offer optimal solutions and pursue sustainable value through strict quality management and ethical management.

Products & Services

Electronics & Accessories

We supply cameras, mirrorless cameras, smartphones, game consoles and accessories. We offer only high-quality products that have undergone strict inspection and certification, and provide product comparisons to help customers choose the best product for their needs.

Agricultural Products

We carefully export fresh apples, pears, plums and traditional Korean foods such as chilli powder, sesame oil, perilla oil and black goat extract. We comply with food safety certifications and maintain freshness with rapid cold chain logistics.

Comprehensive Trade Services

We support the entire trade process from product sourcing and export agency to logistics, customs clearance, trade consulting and market research. With our professional personnel and global network, we guide your business to success.
