About Us

BlauArk is an export‑specialized company established as a trusted trade partner connecting Korea and the world, as its name “Ark on the Blue Sea” suggests. We supply a diverse range of products to global markets—from renowned brands such as Nikon, Canon and Sony cameras and mirrorless cameras, Apple iPhones and Samsung Galaxy smartphones, and console gaming devices, to premium Korean fruits like apples, pears and plums, red pepper powder, sesame and perilla oils, and various lifestyle goods.

With thorough quality inspections and a responsible supply chain, we ensure safe transactions. Collaborating with global partners, we offer tailored trade solutions including export/import agency, logistics and customs clearance, market research and consulting to support our clients’ business success. We pursue sustainable value and innovation, aiming to be the Ark of trade that grows together with customers through transparent communication and rapid response.

Products & Services

Electronics & IT

We supply cameras, mirrorless cameras, smartphones, console gaming devices, and accessories from leading global brands. Through strict quality checks we offer only genuine, high‑quality products, provide the latest lineup and ensure thorough after‑sales care.

Fruits & Agricultural Products

We export premium Korean fruits such as apples, pears and plums, as well as red pepper powder, sesame oil, perilla oil and other traditional foods. We maintain freshness through rigorous quality control and hygiene inspections, complying with HACCP and other food safety standards.

Comprehensive Trade Services

We support the entire trade process including product sourcing, export/import agency, logistics and transportation, customs services, trade consulting and market research. Our professional team and global network lead your business to success.
