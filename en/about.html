About

BlauArk is an export‑specialized company established as a trusted trade partner connecting Korea and the world, as its name “Ark on the Blue Sea” suggests. We supply a diverse range of products to global markets—from renowned brands such as Nikon, Canon and Sony cameras and mirrorless cameras, Apple iPhones and Samsung Galaxy smartphones, and console gaming devices, to premium Korean fruits like apples, pears and plums, red pepper powder, sesame and perilla oils, and various lifestyle goods.

Through thorough quality inspections and a responsible supply chain, we ensure safe transactions. Collaborating with global partners, we offer tailored trade solutions including export/import agency, logistics and customs clearance, market research and consulting to support our clients’ business success. We pursue sustainable value and innovation and aim to be the Ark of trade that grows together with customers through transparent communication and rapid response.

Vision · Mission · Core Values

Vision: Our vision is to connect buyers and producers worldwide through trust, growing into a global trade partner. We aim to evolve beyond a mere supplier and become a partner that designs our customers’ success, connecting the world and creating value through trade.

Mission: Our mission is to create value beyond expectations by providing the highest quality products and services and building long‑term trust through transparent communication and prompt response. Through continuous innovation and improvement, we grow together with our customers and enhance the value of trade.

Core Values: Trust, Quality, Innovation and Speed. We place transparency and integrity first, honor our commitments, guarantee perfect quality through comprehensive inspections, swiftly adapt to market changes and seek continuous improvement, and support success through fast and flexible logistics.
